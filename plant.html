Welcome to the World of Plants

What are Plants?

Plants are living organisms that belong to the kingdom Plantae. They produce their own food through a process called photosynthesis, using sunlight, carbon dioxide, and water.

Types of Plants

Plants come in many varieties, including:

  • Trees
  • Shrubs
  • Herbs
  • Climbers
  • Creepers

Why Are Plants Important?

Plants provide us with oxygen, food, and medicine. They are essential for maintaining the balance of the ecosystem and are a crucial part of life on Earth.
